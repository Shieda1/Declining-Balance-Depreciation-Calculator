What is the declinging balance method?
The declining balance is a method of computing depreciation rate for the value of an asset. The declining balance method is also sometimes known as reducing balance method and diminishing balance method.
How to calculate declinging balance method?
To calculate declinging balance method you should use the following formula:
declinging balance method = (net book value - residual value) * rate of debreciation
Why use declinging balance method?
The declining balance method is used because it results in a lower amount of taxable income early in the life of the asset and that means that the associated income taxes are lower in the first years.
